# figgy-ai-gig-worker-insurance
# FIGGY
### AI-Powered Parametric Income Insurance for Gig Workers

FIGGY is an AI-powered parametric insurance platform designed to protect gig workers such as delivery partners from income loss caused by external disruptions like heavy rain, floods, pollution, strikes, or curfews.

Unlike traditional insurance systems that require manual claims, FIGGY automatically detects disruptions and triggers payouts using real-world data and verified worker activity.

The platform also includes AI demand prediction to help workers move to high-demand zones and increase their earnings.

---

# Problem Statement

India’s gig economy includes millions of delivery workers working on platforms like:

- Swiggy
- Zomato
- Zepto
- Amazon

These workers rely on daily deliveries for income. However, external disruptions such as:

- Heavy rainfall
- Flooding
- Severe air pollution
- Curfews
- Local strikes

can reduce their working hours and earnings by **20–30%**.

Currently, gig workers have **no financial protection** against such disruptions.

---

# Our Solution

FIGGY provides an AI-driven parametric insurance system that:

- Predicts demand zones for higher earnings
- Detects disruptions automatically
- Verifies worker activity
- Calculates income loss
- Sends instant insurance payouts

---

# Key Features

## 1. Income Optimization
Predicts high-demand delivery zones 1–2 hours in advance so riders can get more orders.

## 2. Income Protection
Automatically compensates workers when disruptions reduce their earnings.

## 3. Fraud Prevention
Uses proof-of-work verification including GPS movement, activity logs, and delivery attempts.

## 4. Instant Payouts
Insurance claims are triggered automatically and payouts are sent instantly.

# Example Test Use Case

### Scenario: Heavy Rain Causes Income Loss

Worker: Ravi (Swiggy Delivery Partner)  
Location: T Nagar, Chennai

### Expected Earnings
Based on historical data, Ravi normally earns:

₹800 per day

### Actual Earnings (Rain Day)

Due to heavy rain, fewer orders were placed and Ravi earned:

₹250

### Income Loss

Income Loss = Expected Earnings − Actual Earnings

₹800 − ₹250 = ₹550

### Insurance Payout

The system compensates part of the loss.

Insurance Payout = ₹385

### Final Earnings

Actual Earnings + Insurance Payout

₹250 + ₹385 = ₹635

FIGGY reduces Ravi's loss from ₹550 to ₹165.
